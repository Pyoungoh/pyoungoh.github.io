# Pace Lab Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Pace Lab is a record and calculation tool application designed for runners. The App does not provide account registration or login functionality.

## 1. Information We Collect

Pace Lab does not collect or store directly identifiable personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

All running records and calculation data entered by users are stored locally on the user’s device and are not transmitted to any external server.

## 2. Advertising and Third-Party Services

Pace Lab displays advertisements through Google AdMob.

For the purpose of ad delivery and performance measurement, Google may collect certain information, including but not limited to:

- Advertising identifiers (such as IDFA or other device identifiers)
- Device information
- App usage information related to advertisements

This information is processed in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

Google Privacy Policy: 
[https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## 3. Data Storage

Pace Lab does not store user data on external servers.

All data entered by users remains stored locally on the user’s device.

## 4. Tracking and Advertising Identifiers

The App uses a third-party advertising network (Google AdMob), and advertising identifiers (such as IDFA) may be used for ad delivery purposes.

Users can manage advertising tracking permissions through their iOS device settings.
