# Pace Lab Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Pace Lab is a record and calculation tool application designed for runners. The App does not provide account registration or login functionality.

## 1. Information We Collect and Process

Pace Lab does not collect or store directly identifiable personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.
Pace Lab also does not access or collect health data stored on your device, including data from Health Connect, Google Fit, Apple Health, or similar health platforms.
However, for the purpose of providing calculation features and improving usability, some values and settings that you enter directly may be stored locally on your device. Examples may include:

- Calculation inputs entered directly by you, such as weight
- Inputs used for pace, distance, time, and heart rate zone calculations
- Recently used settings and app preferences

This information is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to the developer’s external servers.

## 2. How We Use Information

Pace Lab uses the information you enter only for the following purposes:

- Providing running-related calculation features
- Improving usability, such as keeping recent inputs
- Maintaining app settings and user preferences

Pace Lab does not use the information you enter for advertising or marketing purposes.

## 3. Advertising and Third-Party Services

Pace Lab may display advertisements through Google AdMob.
For ad delivery and performance measurement, Google and other third-party service providers may process certain information in accordance with applicable laws and their own policies, including:

- Advertising identifiers
- Device information
- App usage information related to advertisements

This information is processed according to the policies of the third-party service providers, and is not separately collected and stored by the developer of Pace Lab.

Google Privacy Policy:
[https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## 4. Data Retention

Pace Lab does not store the data and settings entered by users on the developer’s external servers.
Data and settings entered by users are stored only locally on the user’s device and may remain there until the user deletes the data or deletes the App.

## 5. How to Delete Data

Users can delete locally stored app data in the following ways:

- Use an in-app reset or clear-input feature, if available
- Delete app storage or app data from the device settings
- Delete the App

Because Pace Lab does not store user data on external servers, locally stored information will also be removed when the App is deleted or the app data is cleared.

## 6. Sharing and Sale of Data

Pace Lab does not transmit user-entered calculation data or settings to the developer’s external servers, and does not sell or share such data with third parties.

However, information processed by third-party services such as Google AdMob during ad delivery may be handled in accordance with those providers’ own policies.

## 7. Children’s Privacy

Pace Lab is not designed primarily for children, and the App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in applicable laws, app features, or third-party services. If significant changes are made, the updated policy will be posted on this page.
